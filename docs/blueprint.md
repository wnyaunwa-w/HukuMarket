# **App Name**: HukuMarket

## Core Features:

- User Authentication: Securely register and authenticate buyers and sellers.
- Listing Management: Allow sellers to create, update, and manage their broiler chicken listings with details like quantity, breed, and price.
- Search and Filtering: Enable buyers to search for broiler chickens based on location, breed, price range, and quantity.
- Direct Messaging: Facilitate direct communication between buyers and sellers for inquiries and negotiations.
- Location Services: Display seller locations on a map to help buyers find nearby broiler chicken providers.
- Reviews and Ratings: Enable buyers to rate and review sellers, building trust and transparency in the marketplace.
- AI powered Listing Improvement: Use an LLM as a tool to suggest listing improvements that may include better text to advertise the offering, as well as appropriate pricing suggestions based on historical sales data.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Earthy yellow (#B8860B), reflecting the natural aspect of poultry farming.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC), offering a clean and neutral backdrop.
- Accent color: Olive green (#6B8E23), complementing the primary color and representing growth and freshness.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, for a balance of modern clarity and approachability.
- Use clean, modern icons related to poultry, farming, and location. Simple and easily recognizable.
- Clean and intuitive layout, prioritizing clear product listings and easy navigation.
- Subtle animations for loading states and interactions to enhance user experience.
